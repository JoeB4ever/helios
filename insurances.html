Insurances

We accept most insurance plans, including Medicare and some Medicaid plans.

In the event that your insurance does not cover services, we promise to work with the individual financial needs of each patient

and ensure that you are able to receive the services you want and need as we do have cash services available
Services may be covered in full or in part by your health insurance.

Please check your coverage by asking the following questions:

  • What is my health insurance coverage and benefits?
  • Am I within network?
  • What is my deductible and has it been met?
  • Do I have a copay or coinsurance? And if so, what is it?
