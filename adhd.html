What is ADHD?

Having trouble with focusing, concentration, or impulsivity?.
Problems with following directions, staying on task, fidgetiness, and acting with out thinking can be due to Attention Deficit Hyperactifity Disorder.

ADHD is offtenly diagnosed in children, but adults who have a hard time being productive at work, time managment, or staying orgianized can aslo have ADHD.
